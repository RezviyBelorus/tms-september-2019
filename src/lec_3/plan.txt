Почему иногда обертки примитивов могут давать "true" при сравнении через "=="
If the value p being boxed is true, false, a byte, or a char in the range \u0000 to \u007f,
or an int or short number between -128 and 127 (inclusive), then let r1 and r2 be
the results of any two boxing conversions of p. It is always the case that r1 == r2.
